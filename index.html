Japanese Kintsugi

Apnaa Addaa Cafe
Titanium City Center, 100 Feet Anand Nagar Rd, Satellite, Ahmedabad, Gujarat 380015

Japanese Kintsugi Experience: Craft with Patience, Learn with Philosophy

Discover the timeless Japanese art of Kintsugi, a practice that transforms broken ceramics into beautiful pieces by celebrating their cracks rather than hiding them.

More than an art activity, Kintsugi is rooted in the philosophy of embracing imperfections, resilience, and growth. Through this guided creative experience, you'll explore the meaning behind this centuries-old tradition while creating a unique piece of your own.

Whether you're looking to unwind, learn something new, or spend an afternoon immersed in creativity, this experience offers a thoughtful blend of art, mindfulness, and culture.

What To Expect

  • Learn the story and philosophy behind Kintsugi and the Japanese concept of finding beauty in imperfection
  • Create your own Kintsugi-inspired ceramic piece using provided materials
  • Enjoy a calming, hands-on creative experience in a welcoming environment
  • Connect with fellow art, culture, and craft enthusiasts
  • Take home a meaningful artwork crafted by you

Who Is It For?

  • Art and craft enthusiasts
  • Beginners with no prior experience
  • People seeking mindful and creative experiences
  • Anyone curious about Japanese culture and philosophy

Important Note

  • All materials will be provided. No prior artistic experience is required.
  • The images used in promotional materials are for representation purposes only. Actual artwork and materials may vary.
Price 1099/- Rs.
